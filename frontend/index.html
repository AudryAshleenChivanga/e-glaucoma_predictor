How It Works

Our AI-powered pipeline for accurate glaucoma detection

01

Upload Image

Upload a high-quality retinal fundus photograph. Our system accepts images from standard ophthalmological equipment in common formats like JPG, PNG, and TIFF.

02

AI Analysis

Our deep learning CNN model with 27 million parameters analyzes the retinal image, examining key features like optic disc changes, cup-to-disc ratio, and nerve fiber patterns.

03

Instant Results

Receive detailed probability scores, risk assessments (Low/Moderate/High), and personalized recommendations for follow-up care within seconds.

Deep Learning

Real-time

Validated

Why Early Detection Matters

Glaucoma is one of the leading causes of irreversible blindness worldwide, affecting over 80 million people. Often called the "silent thief of sight," it typically shows no early symptoms until significant vision loss has occurred.

  • 80+ million people affected globally
  • #1 cause of irreversible blindness
  • 50% of cases go undiagnosed
  • Early detection can prevent 90% of vision loss
